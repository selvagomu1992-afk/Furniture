Our Story

Born in a workshop.
Built for lifetimes.

Jangid began when master carpenter Aldric Voss set up a small workshop in Vermont with a singular obsession: furniture that could outlast its maker.

Today, our team of 23 artisans works across 8,000 sq ft of studio space, blending time-honoured joinery techniques with contemporary design sensibility.

Every piece is made to order. Every order, personal. We don't keep inventory — we keep craft.

How It Works

From concept to creation

01

Configure & Order

Choose your piece, select materials, finishes, and dimensions. Our online configurator gives you a live price estimate — or speak to a designer directly.

02

Crafted by Hand

Your piece enters our workshop queue. A dedicated artisan builds it from scratch using traditional joinery, hand-applied finishes, and premium upholstery.

03

White Glove Delivery

Your furniture is carefully wrapped, shipped with full insurance, and installed by our delivery team. We only leave when you're completely satisfied.

Typical lead time: 6–10 weeks from order confirmation
